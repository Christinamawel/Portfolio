After getting laid off from my job of 5 years I decided to take my learning from an after-work hobby to full-time studying. I have a love for learning and growing and have used that the past year to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Adobe Illustrator, XD, and Photoshop on my own as well as looking into many other front-end development related technologies.

The more I learned the more I discovered how much learning I had left to do and that's what brought me to Epicodus school where I have created over 70 projects, many of which while pair programming with different students. I have also continued to study on my own as well and have begun to learn mobile programming in Android Studio with Kotlin. I have learned a lot since I begun my journey into programming and I feel proud of what I have accomplished, however I will never stop continuing to learn and grow from here.
