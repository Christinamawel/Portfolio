Background

I started out working, like most people, in customer service. I was a drive thru worker for a local burger king, and there I learned how to work fast, efficently and how to deal with stress in the workplace. It was not an easy job, or a fun job, but it taught me a lot and I'm glad I did it.

I then moved on to working for a very wonderful small buissness company called NeoMetal. We manufactured jewlery for piercing shops. I started out polishing but was quickly moved up to order fulfilment and spent my days counting tiny parts to be put into orders. It was a bit tedious but it taught me to double check and triple check everything I did to ensure customers got the correct number and type of pieces. It often would get very busy as well so I learned to quality check carefully very quickly.

Last May the company NeoMetal relocated most of its facilities to California and I found myself without a job. I didnt want to go back to the same job though, so I pushed myself to study throughout the summer and learned everything I could find. Unfortunatly for me there is a lot to learn out there that can be found so I lost focus on what exactly to study. this is what took me to Epicodus school where I'm studying today. I'm hoping through this school I can gain focus to find the sort of carrer I've wanted.
